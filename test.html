Neil deGrasse Tyson

Neil deGrasse Tyson is a world-renowned astrophysicist, political commentator and all-round science champion.

Best known as the man largely responsible for Pluto no longer being a planet, Tyson is director of the Hayden Planetarium and a Research Associate in the Department of Astrophysics in the American Museum of Natural History. He also hosts PBS' NOVA ScienceNOW.

In the event of an impending intergalactic distaster, Tyson is the man you want on your side.

Christopher Hitchens

Christopher Hitchens is widely regarded as one of the leading voices in the atheism movement.

A true man of his conviction, he was once waterboarded to discern whether it was indeed a form of torture. His regular column in Vanity Fair, much like Christopher himself, is unabashedly vitriolic, outspoken and, above all, engaging.

Due to his ongoing battle with esophageal cancer, Christopher is unable to travel and will be appearing at Think Inc. live via video-link from New York.
